Contribution to the Society

(1)Improve Aquaculture Efficiency and Sustainability

Through innovative vaccination technologies, this project significantly enhances the efficiency and sustainability of aquaculture. Field research in Qinhuangdao's fisheries revealed bottlenecks in current large-scale farming practices, particularly efficiency and cost challenges with traditional injection and immersion methods. The automated injection system and continuous immersion inoculation technology developed in this project effectively improve vaccination efficiency, reduce labor costs, and boost overall aquaculture profitability.

(2)Ensure the Quality and Safety of Aquatic Products

This initiative utilizes vaccination technology to reduce disease incidence in aquaculture animals and decrease antibiotic usage, thereby enhancing the quality and safety of aquatic products. For instance, Hainan Wanning Linlan Group has implemented vaccination techniques in Oriental Starfish farming, which has significantly improved fish health and reduced disease transmission risks. This approach not only meets consumers 'demand for healthy and safe aquatic products but also aligns with China's national food safety strategy.

(3)Promote Aquaculture Technology Innovation and Industrial Upgrading

This project fosters innovation and industrial upgrading in aquaculture through corporate partnerships. For instance, collaboration with Linlan Group has not only enhanced breeding techniques for Oriental Starfish (Orient Starfish) but also provided technical references for cultivating other high-value aquatic species. Such partnerships enable enterprises to reduce operational risks, improve product quality, and strengthen market competitiveness.

(4)Promoting Economic Growth and Employment

The implementation of this project will drive the development of related industries and create more job opportunities. For instance, the research and application of vaccine technology require specialized technical personnel and equipment support, which will boost industries such as smart device manufacturing and biotechnology R&D. Meanwhile, efficient breeding techniques will enhance corporate profitability and stimulate local economic growth.

Contribution to the scientific Community

(1)Technical Optimization and Innovation

Through animal experiments on species such as the turbot, we have optimized vaccination techniques to enhance vaccine utilization and immunization efficacy. For instance, in Qinhuangdao Fisheries' trials, our continuous immersion inoculation method was found to significantly improve vaccine uniformity and administration efficiency while reducing fish stress responses. The study also validated the effectiveness of intelligent management systems in water quality monitoring and disease prevention, providing aquaculture enterprises with a comprehensive smart solution.

(2)Data and Experience Accumulation

Animal experiments have accumulated extensive experimental data and practical experience, providing scientific foundations for further development of vaccination technologies. For instance, in the experiment conducted by Hainan Wanning Linlan Group, we collected immunological response data following vaccination with the Oriental Star Spotted Fish vaccine, which served as crucial references for vaccine R&D optimization. These data and insights not only enhance vaccine performance but also offer valuable lessons for developing vaccines for other fish species.

(3)Market and Consumer Demand Satisfaction

Through strategic partnerships with enterprises, this initiative effectively addresses market demands and consumer expectations for premium aquatic products. By implementing vaccination technologies to enhance product quality and safety standards, it meets growing consumer needs for wholesome and secure seafood. Furthermore, through technical collaborations, the project empowers businesses to elevate product excellence, strengthen market competitiveness, and successfully meet the rising demand for high-value aquatic products.

Sharing of Output Results and Potential Benefits

(1)Sharing with the Enterprise

The project's outcomes have been shared with multiple enterprises, including Qinhuangdao Fisheries and Hainan Wanning Linlan Group. Through technical training, seminars, and field research, we have engaged in in-depth exchanges and collaborations with companies regarding vaccination technologies and intelligent management systems. For instance, during the training session at Hainan Wanning Linlan Group, we shared the latest vaccination techniques to help optimize their vaccination processes and enhance breeding efficiency. Additionally, through technical partnerships, we assisted the company in developing new vaccine products to boost their market competitiveness.

(2)Sharing with the Scientific Community

The experimental data and research findings from this project will be shared with the scientific community through academic papers, technical reports, and other publications. These achievements not only provide new data and methodologies for fish immunology research but also offer valuable references for studies in related fields. For instance, the optimized vaccination techniques and application effects of intelligent management systems identified in our experiments will be published in academic papers, providing fresh research perspectives and technical references for the scientific community.

(3)Sharing with the Social Sector

The outcomes of this project will also be shared with the social sector through technology promotion and policy recommendations. For instance, we will conduct technology outreach activities to introduce the advantages and application methods of vaccination techniques to more aquaculture enterprises and farmers, thereby promoting the widespread adoption of these technologies.
